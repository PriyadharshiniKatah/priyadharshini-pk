# Fincare–AU Merger Integration

## Problem

The merger required seamless migration of systems, processes, branding, compliance requirements, and customer-facing operations without disrupting business continuity.

## My Role

Led business-side execution across Connector, Insurance, and Strategic Alliance channels.

## Actions

- Coordinated IT migration and system transitions
- Managed branding and communication changes
- Worked with Compliance, Legal, Operations, and Technology teams
- Identified operational risks and mitigation plans
- Ensured continuity of channel performance during transition

## Results

- Successfully executed transition with minimal business disruption
- Ensured readiness across multiple channels and stakeholders
- Maintained operational continuity throughout integration
- Delivered migration milestones within planned timelines

## Key Learnings

Large-scale transformations succeed through stakeholder alignment, structured execution, and proactive risk management.
