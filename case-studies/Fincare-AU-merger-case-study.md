# Fincare–AU Merger Integration: Leading Business-Critical Channel & Technology Migration

## Overview

Following the merger of Fincare Small Finance Bank with AU Small Finance Bank, I led the integration and migration of multiple business channels, technology workflows, partner ecosystems, and operational processes across the Affordable Housing Loan (AHL) and Loan Against Property (LAP) businesses.

Having ownership of the Connector, Insurance, and Strategic Alliances channels, along with several technology transformation initiatives, I became responsible for ensuring seamless migration of channel operations, partner relationships, business processes, customer journeys, reporting structures, and CRM-related workflows into the AU ecosystem.

The objective was to achieve business continuity while minimizing operational disruption and ensuring compliance with regulatory, audit, technology, and branding requirements.

---

## The Challenge

The merger presented several complex challenges:

- Migration of business processes across two independent organizations.
- Alignment of different CRM systems and customer data structures.
- Migration of channel-specific workflows and operating models.
- Rebranding of customer-facing and partner-facing assets.
- Integration of Insurance, Connector, and Strategic Alliance ecosystems.
- Alignment of compliance, legal, audit, and operational requirements.
- Preservation of business continuity during migration.
- Training teams on new systems and processes.

The risk of operational disruption, data inconsistency, customer dissatisfaction, and channel performance decline was significant if migration activities were not executed effectively.

---
