# Scaling Connector Network from 3,200 to 26,000+

## Problem

The connector channel had limited penetration and inconsistent engagement.

## My Role

Owned growth strategy, partner onboarding, engagement programs, analytics, and stakeholder management.

## Actions

- Onboarded broker groups and DSA networks
- Built engagement programs
- Introduced contests and incentive programs
- Developed KPI dashboards
- Optimized acquisition and retention funnels

## Results

- Partner base grew from 3,200 to 26,000+
- Channel penetration increased from 32% to 69%
- Market penetration improved by 37%

## Key Learnings

Growth is driven by activation and retention, not just acquisition.
