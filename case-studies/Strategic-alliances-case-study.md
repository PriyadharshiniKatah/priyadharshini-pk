# Building Strategic Alliances for Revenue Growth

## Problem

The organization had limited partnership-driven acquisition channels and untapped B2B growth opportunities.

## My Role

Owned alliance identification, onboarding, relationship management, and business growth initiatives.

## Actions

- Identified high-potential B2B partners
- Built partnership proposals and business cases
- Led onboarding and implementation discussions
- Established governance and performance review mechanisms
- Worked with product teams to align offerings with partner needs

## Results

- Built and expanded strategic alliance network
- Drove 17% profitability growth through targeted partnerships
- Improved cross-functional collaboration across business units
- Created sustainable revenue streams through long-term partnerships

## Key Learnings

Strong partnerships are built on aligned incentives, measurable outcomes, and continuous engagement.
