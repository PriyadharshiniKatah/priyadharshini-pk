# Insurance Channel Transformation

## Overview

At AU Small Finance Bank, I managed the Insurance Channel across partnerships with ICICI Lombard and Kotak. While the channel had strong business potential, only 62% of eligible customers were opting for insurance products.

The journey was heavily dependent on manual documentation, fragmented workflows, and strict insurer turnaround requirements. This resulted in high customer drop-offs, repeated rework, delayed processing, and lost business opportunities.

Over an 18-month period, I led the transformation of the insurance journey through process redesign, digitization, stakeholder alignment, and customer experience improvements.

---

## The Challenge

Key challenges included:

- Only 62% insurance conversion among eligible customers.
- Manual documentation and verification processes.
- Minor documentation errors required complete rework of applications.
- More than 40% of cases required rework.
- Strict insurer turnaround timelines led to high fallout rates.
- Customers were often unwilling to revisit branches to resubmit documents.
- Legacy unresolved cases continued to impact channel performance.

The result was poor customer experience, long processing cycles, and missed revenue opportunities.

---

## My Approach

### Root Cause Analysis

I mapped the end-to-end insurance journey and identified major drop-off points across documentation, verification, submission, and insurer processing stages.

### Process Digitization

I partnered with Product, Technology, Operations, Business, and Insurance teams to redesign the process and reduce manual intervention.

Key initiatives included:

- Digitization of documentation workflows.
- Standardized submission processes.
- Reduced dependency on physical paperwork.
- Introduced validation checkpoints before submission.
- Improved visibility and tracking throughout the application lifecycle.

### Stakeholder Management

I worked closely with:

- ICICI Lombard
- Kotak
- Technology teams
- Operations teams
- Branch teams
- Business stakeholders

to improve accountability, collaboration, and turnaround times.

### Recovery Campaigns

In parallel, I launched targeted campaigns to revisit and recover previously unresolved insurance opportunities and improve overall channel contribution.

---

## Outcomes

### Business Impact

- Increased insurance conversion from 62% to 83%.
- Reduced average turnaround time from 65 days to 21 days.
- Reduced rework rates from over 40% to 7%.
- Recovered and converted a portion of previously abandoned opportunities.
- Increased insurance penetration and revenue contribution.

### Customer Impact

- Faster policy issuance and onboarding.
- Reduced customer effort and branch revisits.
- Lower documentation burden.
- Improved customer satisfaction and trust.

### Organizational Impact

- Established a scalable and repeatable digital insurance process.
- Improved collaboration across internal and external stakeholders.
- Created a stronger foundation for future channel growth and automation.
