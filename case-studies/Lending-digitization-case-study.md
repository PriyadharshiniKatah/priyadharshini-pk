# End-to-End Digitization of SBL & AHL Loan Journeys

## Problem

The loan journey involved multiple manual interventions, resulting in longer turnaround times, operational inefficiencies, and inconsistent customer experiences.

## My Role

Acted as the business owner driving digitization across Product, Technology, Operations, Compliance, and Business teams.

## Actions

- Mapped the complete customer journey from onboarding to disbursal
- Identified manual bottlenecks and process inefficiencies
- Prioritized technology enhancements and workflow automation
- Coordinated execution across IT, Operations, and business stakeholders
- Managed testing, rollout, and adoption across channels

## Results

- Reduced turnaround time across loan processing stages
- Improved operational efficiency by eliminating manual dependencies
- Enhanced customer experience through a streamlined digital journey
- Increased scalability for future business growth

## Key Learnings

Successful digitization requires alignment between business objectives, customer needs, and technology execution.
